John Willis
Chief Software Architect

John, a New Mexico native, has worked in the Information Technology field at a number of local businesses, including Elephant Butte Irrigation District and Ariel Technologies. He has been employed as a Systems Administrator at Lockheed Martin, and as a Software Engineer for General Dynamics. He has spent his time in recent years developing custom software solutions using the .NET Framework and C, and has been known to make the occasional foray into assembly language.

John holds professional certifications in his field from Microsoft, NASA, and Sun Microsystems.
